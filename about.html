G&G

grapes and gasoline

Grapes and Gasoline is a brand created with, by, and because of passion. Two things, that don´t fit together in any way. Yet they have found a way in the same place. The grapes represent life, they are life, they grow, they are nourishment. After harvest, made into the most complex drink ever designed. Infinite combinations to bring the calm of nature in an expression of pure passion and delight. Next to gasoline, derived from black gold, ruling every single corner of the Earth, the power of a million horses trampling the steppes of freedom. Powering what man gives his own personal bit of nature´s strength. These are the two raw components that bring out the passion in a person. Put simple: wine and motorcycles. G&G is focussing on building a brand of its own. To do things we love, our focus is just like our name, very wide and perhaps not fitting together. That´s not what we think. Photography and Graphic Design is the first focus. We specialise in product, commercial and advertising photography. In addition to that, we do all your graphic design work, following up on our photography. Horeca is our second focus. As we try to expand our horizon in the wine business. This includes bars and restaurants, as well as future plans to import and distribute. If you have anything to ask, refer to our contact form.
